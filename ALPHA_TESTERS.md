# KidQuest Alpha — Quick Start for Testers

Welcome! You're helping shape a learning app for curious kids (ages 6–14).

**Try it now:** https://kidquest-indol.vercel.app

---

## 1. Get in (parents)

1. Open the link above → tap **Let's get started**.
2. **Create account** (invite code if you have one) or **Sign in**.
3. Set up your child's name, avatar, and age on the onboarding screen.
4. Default parent PIN is `1234` — change it the first time you open **Settings** (Me tab → Settings).

---

## 2. What to try first (kids)

1. **Home** — tap the big **Play!** button or pick a world (Geography, Math, Science, Trivia, Curiosity).
2. **Geography** — complete one track (flags or map locator are fan favorites).
3. **Math** — try **Multiplication Camp** → pick a times table → **Practice**.
4. **Curiosity Hub** — read today's spark and tap **Save for later**.
5. **My Journey** — see progress across subjects.

---

## 3. Tell us what broke

- Tap the **Send feedback** button (bottom-right on most pages).
- Pick a category, write what happened, and submit.
- Or email issues to the team contact from **About us**.

---

## 4. Good to know

- **100% free**, no ads, works in the browser (add to home screen on mobile).
- Progress saves on this device; sign in to sync when cloud is enabled.
- **Quest Home** (floating button) appears during lessons — tap it to get back to Home.
- Some worlds say **Coming soon** — that's expected in alpha.
